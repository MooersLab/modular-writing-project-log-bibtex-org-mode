# Nine questions addressed to generate a story summary.
1. Introduction: What is the central question?
2. Introduction: Why is the question important?
3. Introduction: What data(variables) are needed to answer this question?
4. Methods: What methods are used to get those data?
5. Methods: What analysis must be applied for the data to answer the central question?
6. Results: What data (values) were obtained?
7. Results: What were the results of the analyses?
8. Discussion: How did the analyses answer the central question?
9. Discussion: What does this answer tell use about the broader field?
