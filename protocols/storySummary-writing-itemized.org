# Nine questions addressed to generate a story summary.
- Introduction: What is the central question?
- Introduction: Why is the question important?
- Introduction: What data(variables) are needed to answer this question?
- Methods: What methods are used to get those data?
- Methods: What analysis must be applied for the data to answer the central question?
- Results: What data (values) were obtained?
- Results: What were the results of the analyses?
- Discussion: How did the analyses answer the central question?
- Discussion: What does this answer tell use about the broader field?
